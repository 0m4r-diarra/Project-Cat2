Financial Tips & Literacy

Learn smart habits to maximize your income.

Budgeting
The 50/30/20 Rule

Allocate 50% of your income to needs (rent, groceries), 30% to wants (entertainment), and securely save the remaining 20%.

Debt
Avoid Mobile Loan Traps

High-interest quick loans can destroy your budget. Build an emergency fund so you don't have to rely on expensive credit.

Growth
Automate Your Savings

Treat your savings like a mandatory monthly bill. Set up an automatic transfer on payday before you have the chance to spend it.
