Financial Tips & Literacy

Learn smart habits to maximize your income and reach your milestones.

Small Actions, Huge Gains

Building wealth isn't about making a million dollars overnight. It's about consistency, mastering control over small impulses, and letting your savings grow compound interest over time. Dive into our updated playbook below.

Budgeting
The 50/30/20 Rule

Allocate 50% of your income to needs (rent, groceries), 30% to wants (entertainment), and securely save the remaining 20%.

Debt
Avoid Mobile Loan Traps

High-interest quick loans can destroy your budget. Build an emergency fund so you don't have to rely on expensive credit.

Growth
Automate Your Savings

Treat your savings like a mandatory monthly bill. Set up an automatic transfer on payday before you have the chance to spend it.

Security
The 3-6 Month Buffer

Strive to keep at least 3 to 6 months of basic living expenses in a highly liquid account. This ensures unexpected shocks won't derail you.

Tracking
Audit Your Micro-Spends

Daily premium coffee, unmonitored streaming subscriptions, and dining out add up fast. Track your expenses for one week—the data might surprise you.

Investing
Start Early, Scale Slowly

Time in the market beats timing the market. Even small amounts invested consistently in safe funds yield substantial long-term results through compound interest.
