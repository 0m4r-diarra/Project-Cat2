Creating Your Savings Goals

The foundational step to financial freedom starts with clear, intentional parameters.

Setting up your milestone means assigning specific, realistic targets to individual objectives instead of just saving money generic terms. In this module, you specify your goal category—whether it's business capital, an emergency cushion, school tuition, or personal equipment—alongside your target valuation in KES.

By transforming your abstract desires into actionable configurations, the system generates dedicated tracking units for your dashboard. This structured layout breaks large financial mountains down into clear, measurable components that serve as absolute targets for your monthly income.
